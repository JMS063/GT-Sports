Me and My Passion

Hello, my name is minghao Dong and I am a third year BAC student.
For this web design assignment, I chose a racing car related theme.
My inspiration came from a racing game called "GT-Sports". I am a car fanatic.
This game has a very high degree of restoration of the car and the track.
The production and physical parameters of the car in the game are almost exactly
the same as those in reality. At the same time, it has a highly restored track.
So I decided to combine the game with reality and make a racing-themed
webpage to let more people know about racing cars, tracks and events,
and hope that more people can know about the game "GT - Sports".
My preliminary plan for this website is to include cars from all over the world
and some custom-made vehicles for the competition,including real tracks, event information, teams,
event sponsors,and racing pictures and videos based on games.
At the same time, it will also include an introduction section and a feedback section for the game.
hese two sections will not be specially exhibited, and will be displayed in the form of links at the bottom of the page.
